About

The person
behind the work

I come from banking and finance — a world where precision isn't optional, decisions have real consequences, and efficiency directly affects the bottom line. That foundation shapes how I approach every engagement.

Through startup consulting, I learned to move quickly without cutting corners — identifying where a business actually loses time and money, then building targeted solutions rather than layered complexity.

I believe the best technology rarely makes the front page. It just quietly works — saving your team an hour a day, eliminating a manual process, or surfacing the data you didn't know you needed. That's what I build.

Not every problem needs AI. Not every workflow needs a platform. But every business deserves someone who takes the time to understand the problem before proposing a solution.
